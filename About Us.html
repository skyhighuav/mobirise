About Us

SkyHigh Pte Ltd provides an efficient and cost effective solution for all applications of aerial surveying and industrial applications in construction, environmental, oil and gas, plantation. From mine site and large scale topographic surveys to environmental monitoring, we safely deliver highly detailed and accurate spatial data for our clients.

SkyHigh specialises in building customised UAV systems (UAS) with the latest technology in sensors, delivering highly versatile UAS for a wide range of applications.

Our team comprises of UAV builders, engineers, and business developers ensure the best value and delivery for our clients.
